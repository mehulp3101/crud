SCP

The SCP Foundation is a fictional organization. It is a collection of the internet-based fiction project of the same name. The SCP Foundation is responsible for locating and containing individuals, aliens, locations, and objects that violate natural law. These include SCP-173, a killer sculpture that moves when you are not looking, SCP-106, a corpse-like old man who can move through walls and kidnap people into a pocket universe, and SCP-682, a hostile reptile-like creature that seems impossible to destroy. Other SCPs include a being that looks like a plague doctor who creates zombies, a drink machine that can give almost any liquid asked for, and a toaster that can only be talked or written about in first-person.
